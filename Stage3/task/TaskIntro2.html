Allocation of Investment Budgets

Either you or your match will receive a budget of 300 tokens and the other will receive 100 tokens.

{{ if player.treatment == 1 }} One of the gender groups (male or female) will be randomly selected. The 300 token budget will be allocated to the individual (you or your match) who belongs to the selected group. The individual belonging to the other group will receive a budget of 100 tokens. {{ endif }} {{ if player.treatment == 2 }} The 300 token budget will be allocated to the individual who belongs to the group that, on average, invested the most tokens in a previous study. {{ endif }} {{ if player.treatment == 3 }} A real participant recruited from Prolific (the decision maker) will decide on the allocation of budgets. The decision maker is financially incentivized to allocate the 300 token budget to the individual they predict will invest more tokens. To make this prediction, the decision maker is shown the average investments of a male group and a female group in a previous study. {{ endif }}

{{ if player.treatment > 1 }} The male and female groups were labelled as group A and group B respectively to the decision maker. Therefore, they were not aware that these groups corresponded to gender. {{ endif }}

Next Steps

Before moving on to the last page of instructions and learning how many tokens you have been allocated, you must answer the following questions to check your understanding of the instructions up to now. You will earn $0.50 by answering these questions correctly.

This $0.50 is an extra participation fee and is not added to your investment budget. This extra participation fee may be affected by the decisions of yourself or your match later in the experiment.
