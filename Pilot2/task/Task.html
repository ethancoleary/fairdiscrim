Your task is now to allocate investment budgets to individual A and individual B. You must choose one individual to give 300 tokens to and the other will receive 100 tokens. They will then choose how much of this budget to invest in the lottery.

You will earn a commission of 1 cent for every 5 tokens invested by the individual who you assign 300 tokens to. This means that the more invested by the individual who you give 300 tokens to, the more you earn in commission.

Recall that, based on their responses to our questionnaire, individual A belongs to the high investment group: group A, and individual B belongs to the low investment group: group B.

How would you like to allocate the investment budgets?

Allocation 1 Allocation 2
Individual A
(from high investment group) 		300 100
Individual B
(from low investment group) 		100 300


Your decision may be extended to more individuals from the same groups. If so, then the average investments made by all of these individuals will determine your commission.
