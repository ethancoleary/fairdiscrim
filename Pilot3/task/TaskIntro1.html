Investment Instructions

Soon each of you will be given some tokens as an investment budget. Each token has a real dollar value of 1 cent. You will need to decide how many tokens from your investment budget to invest in the following lottery.

Lottery
A fair six-sided die will be rolled. If the die lands on a 1 or a 2, you will win two and half times the amount you invest. If the die lands on a number higher than 2 (3, 4, 5 or 6), you will lose the amount you invest.

Your bonus payment will be equal to the dollar worth of the tokens you do not invest plus any earnings from the lottery.

The die is rolled independently for each individual. This means that your success does not guarantee your match's success and vice versa.
